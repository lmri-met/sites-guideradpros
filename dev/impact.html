Impact

Communication and dissemination of the technical and scientific results of the 22NRM07 GuideRadPROS EMP project is a fundamental part of the project. As of today, the 22NRM07 GuideRadPROS EMP project has produced 3 official documents and 9 contributions to national and international conferences. Additionally, 4 events related to the project have been held.

First Stakeholder Committee meeting of GuideRadPROS

9th February 2024

The first Stakeholder Committee meeting was held in a virtual platform on the 27th February 2024. The agenda of the meeting included an introduction of the project, presentations of stakeholders and a discussion with feedback for the progress of the project consortium.

GuideRadPROS technical visit

9th February 2024

Short technical visit at the x-ray laboratories of ENEA (Casaccia, Italy) in order spectrometry of ISO 4037 standard x-ray beam qualities.

M9 GuideRadPROS meeting

7-8 February 2024

The M9 meeting of the 22NRM07 GuideRadPROS Project was held in Italy at Ospedale Bambino Gesù (Rome).

1st kick off meeting of GuideRadPROS Consortium

10-11 July 2023

The 1st meeting of the EURAMET EMP 22NRM07 GuideRadPROS Consortium took place in Finland at STUK (Vantaa).

Conferences

See more

IRPA 16 HPS

7-12 July 2024, USA

Contribution to the 16th International Congress of the International Radiation Protection Association (IRPA 16 HPS), to be held in Orlando, Florida.

CS LNE 2023

November 2023, France

Contribution to the Annual Meeting of the French Metrology Network for ionizing radiation, held in Cadarache, France, in November 2023. An oral presentation was given at the conference.

44 DRO 2023

6-10 November 2023, Czechia

Two contributions to the 44th Radiation Protection Days annual meeting (44 DRO), held in Tábor, Czechia. An oral presentation and a poster were presented at the conference (see the Book of abstracts).

ICDA-4

16-20 October 2023, Spain

Contribution to the 4th International Conference on Dosimetry and its Applications (ICDA-4), held in Valencia, Spain. A poster was presented in the Individual Dosimetry and Monitoring section (see the Book of abstracts).
