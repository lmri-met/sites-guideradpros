Stakeholder Committee

GuideRadPROS Stakeholder Committee has been established to consult and inform stakeholders about the requirements and project outcomes during the project period. In order to accomplish this, GuideRadPROS shall organise stakeholder meetings to identify problems and propose improvements to the agreed tasks and activities. The following stakeholders contribute to the project as part of the stakeholder committee:

International organizations

ISO - International Organization for Standardization
IEC - International Electrotechnical Commission
EURADOS - European Radiation Dosimetry Group
EMN-RP - European Metrology Network for Radiation Protection

National organizations

BfS - German Federal Office For Radiation Protection
IRSN - L'Institut de Radioprotection et de Sûreté Nucléaire
SSM - Swedish Radiation Safety Authority
DSA - Norwegian Radiation and Nuclear Safety Authority
DZZ - Radiation Protection Society of Serbia and Montenegro
SMU - Slovak Institute of Metrology
