Stakeholder Committee

GuideRadPROS Stakeholder Committee has been established to consult and inform stakeholders about the requirements and project outcomes during the project period. In order to accomplish this, GuideRadPROS shall organise stakeholder meetings to identify problems and propose improvements to the agreed tasks and activities. The following stakeholders contribute to the project as part of the stakeholder committee:
