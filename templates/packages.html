WP1: Validation of requirements and methods related to reference fields according to ISO 4037 and guide for spectrometry

The aim is to evaluate the challenges posed by the ISO 4037 standard series on reference fields, to test the stated requirements and methods related to matched and characterised fields, and to provide the guidance in spectrometry needed for characterised fields.

Tasks

  • Update on ISO 4037 requirements on reference fields
  • Comparison of dosimetric and spectrometric determination of conversion coefficients
  • Preparation of a spectrometry guide

WP2: Training on requirements of ISO 4037 and calibration in reference fields

The aim is to identify actual training needs regarding the implementation of ISO 4037 standard series for setting up new X-ray reference radiation fields and future changes due to ICRU Report 95.

Tasks

  • Develop theoretical training material and testing it in a workshop
  • Develop practical training content and testing in a workshop
  • Develop open-data e-learning material

WP3: Harmonisation and update of type testing standards

The aim is to review existing standards which are the basis for type testing of radiation protection dosimeters and evaluate the need for an update. Different kinds of active and passive photon dosimeters based on different technologies will be investigated.

Tasks

  • Evaluate type testing standards and legal requirements
  • Overview of existing radiation protection dosimeters and current state of the art
  • Harmonisation and update of testing standards

WP4: New technologies and quantities

The aim is to assess future standardisation needs related to new and upcoming technologies and to produce a guidance document for the implementation of the new operational quantities of ICRU 95 into standards and regulations.

Tasks

  • Evaluation of state-of-the-art and upcoming technologies
  • Impact of ICRU 95
